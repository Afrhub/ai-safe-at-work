Partner Programme Overview

MSPs are the primary route to market for AI Safe@Work. You own the customer relationship, the IT and security conversation and the trusted-advisor role. We bring the training, the governance documents and the standards depth. Bundle them into managed IT, cyber, Microsoft 365 or compliance offerings and lift contract value without heavy delivery overhead.

Start: MSP / IT Admin track (provider-side baseline, 45 min) · 30-day rollout guide (the plan you hand each customer).

MSP page is a draft (noindex) until the commercial entity, payment processor and partner agreement are in place. Commercial terms are shared with approved partners in the partner portal.

The programme is a discount plus rebate model: there is no subscription and no monthly platform fee. You buy at a partner discount and earn volume rebates on the business you bring in, paid in arrears and applied retroactively across the year. Full tiers, wholesale pricing and margin figures are shared with approved partners in the partner portal.

Partner Benefits

Four things make the programme worth carrying: real reseller margin on every seat and pack, free not-for-resale licences for internal use, deal registration that protects your pipeline, and co-branded marketing you can put your own logo on.

Margin on every seat and pack

A partner discount on every Course seat, stacking volume rebates, and margin on every Governance Pack deployment. There is no subscription cost at any tier, so the discount is pure margin. Tier-by-tier figures are in the partner portal.

Free NFR licences

Every partner gets free not-for-resale (NFR) licences for internal use: run the Course on your own staff, self-certify before client conversations, and demo the Governance Foundation Pack to prospects. NFR licences cannot be resold or assigned to client accounts. The allowance scales with your tier.

Deal registration

Register a new client opportunity through the partner portal before first client contact. Registered deals are protected for 90 days: within that window you keep exclusive pricing and rebate-tier eligibility on the opportunity, and no other partner can register or undercut it. Registration takes the client name and an estimated seat count, and protection renews automatically while the deal is recorded as active and advancing.

Co-branding

Put your own logo alongside ours. The pitch deck and one-pagers are co-brandable, so you can lead client conversations under your brand while the training and governance content stay ours to maintain. See Marketing Resources for the assets.

Health Check & Consultancy

The consultancy work sits outside the seat-based tiers, since it is project-priced rather than per-user. You do not deliver it directly: you refer qualified clients and earn a referral fee on the first engagement value when a client moves into AI Governance Health Check, gap analysis, governance implementation, or ISO 42001 / ISO 27001 readiness work.

Become a Partner

  1. Take the MSP / IT Admin track and self-certify with your NFR seats.
  2. Register your first opportunities in the partner portal to lock in 90-day protection. The first partner to register a given client opportunity holds it, deal protection is awarded to the first-registered deal.
  3. Email hello@attest-ai.com (subject "MSP partner enquiry") with your MSP name and portfolio size to activate your partner account, or see the contact routes.
