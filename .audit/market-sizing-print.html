TL;DR

The numbers, in one breath.

  • Total addressable market (any AI-using employer globally): ~10M orgs, not useful to monetise.
  • Serviceable addressable market (EU+UK SMB/mid-market that touches AI for work, regulated reach): ~2.5M orgs.
  • Serviceable obtainable (3-year): 1,500–3,000 paying orgs + 5,000–10,000 paying individuals.
  • Y3 ARR, realistic: £250k–£600k self-sustaining · £1–3M with paid marketing or partnership.
  • Y5 ceiling with Qualiopi + accreditation + sector overlays: £8–15M ARR.

Market is real, large, and underserved on the wedge. Bottleneck is founder-time + procurement-readiness gates, not demand.

One-line answer

Realistic addressable market: 2.5M EU+UK SMBs that touch AI for work.

Realistic 3-year win: 1,500–3,000 paying orgs + 5,000–10,000 paying individuals.

Realistic Y3 ARR: £250k–£600k self-sustaining · £1–3M with paid marketing or partnership.

Y5 ceiling w/ Qualiopi + accreditation + sector overlays: £8–15M ARR.

Demand is not the constraint. Procurement-readiness gates + founder time are.
