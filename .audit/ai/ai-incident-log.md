# AI incident log

> **Reg basis:** EU AI Act Art 73 (provider obligation, but useful as deployer too) · ISO/IEC 42001 Annex A.9 · **Owner:** Founder · **Effective:** 2026-05-19

Mirror of the general `../security/incident-log.md` but scoped to AI-specific incidents: hallucinations that shipped, AI-driven decisions later overturned, vendor changes affecting our AI use, prompt-injection or output-handling issues, model behaviour deltas.

## Format

Each entry:

- **ID:** `AINC-YYYY-NN`
- **Detected:** datetime
- **Category:** hallucination-shipped / vendor-behaviour-change / vendor-incident / output-handling / prompt-injection / training-data-concern / model-deprecation
- **Severity:** low / medium / high / critical
- **Reported to authority?** Yes/No + when
- **Summary**
- **Root cause**
- **Actions taken**
- **Lessons captured**
