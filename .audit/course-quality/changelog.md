## v2026.05 — Initial release (2026-05-19)

**Highlights**

- 12 universal modules (M1–M12) covering: why this course exists, what AI tools do with data, the never-paste list, picking the right tool, verifying outputs, AI-powered scams, bias and fairness, copyright and IP, logging, incident response, the 60-second checklist, the standards.
- 2 role tracks: Manager (`module-manager.html`), DPO / compliance lead (`module-dpo.html`).
- 2 templates: Acceptable Use Policy (`templates/aup-template.html`), AI vendor diligence questionnaire (`templates/vendor-questionnaire.html`).
- Standards Map (`standards-map.html`) — 6 standards, 57 clause-to-module rows, 88 cross-link pills.
- Citations bibliography (`citations.html`) — every claim mapped to primary source with confidence flag.
- Changelog page (this).
- Cinematic design system: aurora + drifting orbs + parallax + scroll reveals + reading progress + press feedback.

**Standards baseline at this version**

- EU AI Act — Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 (Art 4 in force 2 Feb 2025; phased through 2026–2027)
- GDPR — Regulation (EU) 2016/679
- ISO/IEC 42001:2023 — AIMS
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — ISMS (Annex A 93 controls)
- NIST AI RMF 1.0 + GAI Profile (NIST AI 600-1)
- OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications (2025 edition)

**Flagged for verification before next release**
