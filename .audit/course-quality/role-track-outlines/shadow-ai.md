# Role-track outline — Shadow AI

> Scope-locking outline. Full content authored at Phase 2+ build slot.

## Audience

Two converging personas:

1. **The CISO / IT lead / security manager** who has discovered that staff are using AI tools the business never approved.
2. **The end-user staff member** who is doing it and does not understand why the business is now asking them to stop.

The track is designed to be deployable to both populations in the same week, with different module entry points and the same shared vocabulary.

## Why a dedicated track

Shadow AI is the #1 operational AI risk in most SMBs per `DOCTRINE.md § Course content v2 → Module 04`. It deserves a deeper-than-one-module treatment because:

- It is **cultural** as much as technical. A policy alone does not solve it; staff have to understand the why.
- It crosses **procurement, security, HR and operations**. Each function has a different angle.
- It interacts with **AI browser extensions, AI SaaS plugins, AI-in-meetings, AI-in-email** — none of which look like ChatGPT but all of which count.

## Standards in scope

EU AI Act Articles 4, 26, 50 · GDPR Articles 5, 6, 28, 32, 33–34, 44–50 · ISO/IEC 42001 Clauses 6, 8 + Annex A.6, A.8, A.10 · ISO/IEC 27001 Annex A.5.10, A.5.23, A.6.3, A.8.12 · NIST AI RMF Govern function.

## Draft module list (8–10 modules)

1. What Shadow AI is — and why it is happening faster than every internal control
2. The four shapes of Shadow AI — consumer chatbots · browser extensions · AI-in-SaaS · personal AI accounts
3. How to discover Shadow AI in your business — proxy logs · SaaS Management Platforms · CASB · paper-and-pen surveys
4. Why a ban does not work — the cultural pattern that defeats every "no AI" policy
5. The acceptable-use posture — what to permit, what to refuse, what to monitor
6. Procurement controls — gating new SaaS tools by AI-feature inventory
7. End-user education — how to explain "use the procured tool" without alienating staff
8. Incident response when Shadow AI causes a data leak
9. The 90-day Shadow AI containment plan — for organisations starting from zero
10. Final assessment
