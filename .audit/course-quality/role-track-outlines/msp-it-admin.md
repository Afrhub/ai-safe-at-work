# Role-track outline — MSP / IT Admin

> Scope-locking outline. Full content authored at Phase 2+ build slot.

## Audience

The MSP technician, IT Admin, or vCIO who is responsible for deploying AI tooling across multiple SMB customers AND for keeping the operational governance posture consistent across all of them. Distinct from the end-user audience — this is the **provider-side** persona.

## Why a dedicated track

Per `DOCTRINE.md § Strategic doctrine + growth framework → Primary route to market — MSP-first`, the MSP is the most leveraged distribution channel. Equipping the MSP technician + admin with a governance-fluent baseline directly multiplies the quality of every downstream customer deployment.

This track is also the **MSP-internal AI literacy** under EU AI Act Article 4 for any MSP that is itself a deployer of AI. The MSP buying this track satisfies its own staff's Article 4 obligation while gaining the operational tooling to satisfy its customers'.

## Standards in scope

EU AI Act Articles 4, 12, 14, 26 (deployer view) · NIST AI RMF Manage function · ISO/IEC 42001 Clauses 7–9 + Annex A.6, A.7, A.9 · ISO/IEC 27001 Annex A.5.10, A.5.23, A.5.24–26, A.6.3, A.8.12, A.8.15 · ITIL 4 AI Operations Governance (when the positioning paper at `DOCTRINE.md § Open strategic TODOs` lands).

## Draft module list (10 modules)

1. MSP as deployer — your Article 4 obligation to your own staff
2. MSP as deployer — your Article 26 obligation to your customers' AI
3. The governance baseline across the MSP customer base
4. Approved tool list — building, maintaining, communicating
5. Customer onboarding — embedding AI governance from day 1 of a managed service
6. Patch + change management for AI tools (Copilot version updates · ChatGPT model swaps · vendor terms changes)
7. Customer audit-pack handover — what the customer keeps, what the MSP keeps
8. Customer incident response — who calls whom in the first ten minutes
9. White-label deployment posture (Strategic tier of the MSP partner programme)
10. Final assessment

## Target audience size (provisional)

- UK + EU MSPs: ~30,000 SMB-focused MSP firms.
- Staff in those firms with technical / admin / vCIO roles: ~150,000.
- Reachable through partner channel + Channel Partner Insight / CRN / IT Europa events.

## Sales motion

- Included in every MSP tier (`msp-tier-feature-matrix.md`).
- Material that the MSP redistributes to its own staff under its standard staff-training process.
- Optional CPD / IT-industry accreditation partnership later.
