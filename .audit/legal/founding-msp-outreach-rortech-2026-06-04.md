- 60-min screen-share walking every role track + overlay
- 30-min walk of the rollout guide as a deliverable structure

  sector specialism

  or a referral-to-consulting customer?

  `msp-tier-feature-matrix.md`

### Weeks 3-4 — First customer kickoff

- Customer-side sponsor identified (with RORtech)
- AUP + training register customised per customer
- Day-1 kickoff message drafted (RORtech sends from customer's sponsor)
- Rollout guide week-1 actions go live

## 7. Customer-pipeline qualification framework

For each customer RORtech brings into the pipeline, classify:

| Dimension | Options |
|---|---|
| **Sector** | FS / Healthcare / Public Sector / Education / Energy / Other |
| **Tier of engagement** | Subscription only / Subscription + governance pack / Subscription + advisory referral / Full advisory engagement |
| **Customer-side AI maturity** | Pre-AI / Shadow-AI-in-use / Tooling procured / Governance partly in place / Audit-ready already |
| **Compliance pressure** | None / Internal driver / Customer pressure / Regulator inquiry / Failed audit |
| **Timing** | This quarter / Next quarter / This year / Speculative |
| **MSP rollout-vs-referral** | RORtech runs the rollout / RORtech refers to AI Safe@Work direct |

Customers scoring "Compliance pressure ≥ customer pressure" + "Timing this quarter" + "Customer-side AI maturity ≤ Tooling procured" are the highest-fit Y1 wins.

## 8. Handover criteria — "actively co-selling" status

RORtech transitions from "Founding cohort onboarded" to "actively co-selling" when:

- [ ] First customer rollout complete + audit binder delivered
- [ ] RORtech account team has completed the MSP / IT Admin track + scored ≥ 8/10 on the assessment
- [ ] At least 2 additional customers in the qualified pipeline
- [ ] First quarterly business review held + actions logged
- [ ] Joint LinkedIn announcement published
- [ ] RORtech has used the rollout guide as a deliverable structure at least twice
