# Pricing decisions — AI Safe@Work

> **Owner:** Founder · **Effective:** 2026-05-19 · **Status:** Draft v1 — not yet live on site · **Review:** quarterly

## Doctrine constraint

Core 12 modules + 2 role tracks + Standards Map + Citations + Changelog stay free. Forever. Wedge depends on it. Free is the trust signal.

Money comes from the operational layer on top: SCORM export, completion certificates with verifiable IDs, knowledge-check scoring, sector overlays, white-label, bespoke content, accredited CPD.

## Individual route

| Tier | Price | Includes | Why this price |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free | £0 | All core content. Self-printed cert (no unique ID). | Wedge defence |
| Pro Certificate | **£49** one-time (€55 / $59) | Knowledge checks · cert w/ unique ID · downloadable transcript · templates pack · lifetime updates | Sits below personal-card approval floor; clears as a meaningful purchase without an approval cycle. 10× cheaper than IAPP AIGP and ISACA AAIA (the only weighty comparables) — credible because we are not accredited yet |
| Pro+ Bundle | **£99** one-time (€115 / $119) | Pro Certificate + sector overlay (finance / healthcare / public-sector) + manager track deep · 12-month email Q&A | Same psychological floor (under £100); adds depth for individuals working in regulated sectors |
| CPD-credited Cert | **£149** one-time (€175 / $179) — *available after accreditation lands* | Pro+ Bundle + IAPP / ISACA / ISC2 CPE credit | Splits credit fee with accreditation body. Higher justified because cert holds professional weight |

## Risks and mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Buyers perceive "free course = no value", paid tiers don't sell | Medium | Paid tiers map to operational evidence (SCORM, audit-pack, scoring), NOT extra content. The free course IS the proof of substance |
| KnowBe4 / IAPP launches sub-£10/seat AI module | Medium | Speed of refresh (quarterly), EU specificity, standards-map depth = harder to copy than price |
| Individual revenue too slow before B2B kicks in | High | Phase-1 sales effort prioritises B2B Starter: 1 Starter sale > 6 individual sales |
| Public sector framework needs Qualiopi + accreditation we don't have | Confirmed | Doctrine § audit-readiness gates 15 + 16 capture this; Phase 4 explicitly gated |
| Charging at all dilutes the "free for everyone" trust | Medium | Free tier never restricted. Paid tiers only add operational artefacts a free user does not need |
| Over-discount on multi-year contracts ruins margin | Low | Discount cap (-15% on 3-year) prevents under-water deals |
