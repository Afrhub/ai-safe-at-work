The course · twelve modules · three role tracks · six templates

The whole course, in one place.

Twelve universal modules, three role tracks (managers, DPOs, MSP / IT admins), six ready-to-print templates, and a standards map for the auditor. Around ninety minutes for the core. Longer if you read the role tracks and templates. No login, no payment.

i
Who this is for

Anyone in a small or medium business who uses ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude, Gemini or any other AI tool for work in sales, marketing, HR, support, ops, finance or comms. You do not need to be technical. If you have ever pasted something into an AI and wondered "should I have done that?", this course covers you.

How to use this course

If you have an hour: read in order. Each module builds on the last.

If you have five minutes: jump to Module 11 — the 60-second checklist. It's the whole course compressed to one page, designed to be printed.

If your manager asked you to "do something about AI compliance": start with Module 12 — the standards, then come back to Module 3 and Module 9.

If you are the manager: start with the 30-day rollout guide.

§
A note on the standards

This course is grounded in ISO/IEC 42001:2023 (AI management systems), ISO/IEC 27001:2022 (information security), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689). We cite them where they're relevant. But this is a practical course for people who use AI tools — not a certification course for auditors. If you need audit-level depth, your compliance team will go deeper.
