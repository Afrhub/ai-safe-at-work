Learning Outcomes

By the end of the core course, you will be able to:

  • Recognise what AI tools do with what you type, and tell free, paid and enterprise tiers apart.
  • Spot the never-paste categories of data and keep them out of public AI tools.
  • Pick the right tool for the job, and know when to refuse a task and ask IT instead.
  • Verify what an AI tells you, catching hallucinations, fake citations and made-up references with the two-source rule.
  • Recognise AI-powered scams, deepfakes and business email compromise, and verify identity when a message reads as perfect.
  • Watch for bias and fairness problems before they embarrass the business.
  • Handle copyright and IP questions around AI output and other people's content.
  • Keep audit-grade records of who used what, and know when an AI-assisted decision needs a human signature.
  • Respond when something goes wrong: escalate, preserve evidence and meet the 72-hour GDPR breach clock.
  • Run the 60-second pre-submit check before pasting anything into an AI tool.
  • Place EU AI Act, ISO 42001, ISO 27001 and GDPR in plain English, and know who to ask for audit-level depth.
