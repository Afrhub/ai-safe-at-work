If your manager asked you to "do something about AI compliance": start with Module 12 — the standards, then come back to Module 3 and Module 9.

If you are the manager: start with the 30-day rollout guide.

§
A note on the standards

This course is grounded in ISO/IEC 42001:2023 (AI management systems), ISO/IEC 27001:2022 (information security), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689). We cite them where they're relevant. But this is a practical course for people who use AI tools — not a certification course for auditors. If you need audit-level depth, your compliance team will go deeper.
