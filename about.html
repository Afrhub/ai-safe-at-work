About us

Making AI safe to use at work.

AI landed in every office faster than the rules could keep up. People paste customer data into tools they have never been told how to use, and the business carries the risk. AI Safe@Work exists to close that gap: train the people, give them the documents to govern it, and help leadership embed it.

Our MissionOur ExperienceMeet the FoundersContact

Our mission

Make safe, lawful, useful AI adoption the default for small and medium businesses and the MSPs who support them. Plain English, UK-first, mapped to the standards that matter, without the jargon or the six-figure consultancy bill.

Our experience

Built by people who work in AI governance, data protection and managed IT every day. Every module and template is grounded in the live regulation, the EU AI Act, GDPR, ISO/IEC 42001 and ISO/IEC 27001, and written for the person who has to act on it, not the lawyer who wrote it. The Standards Map shows exactly where each rule is covered.
