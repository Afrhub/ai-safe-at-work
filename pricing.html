Pricing · Foundation & Platform

Get AI governance ready.

Two ways to buy, priced by the size of your organisation. Foundation gives you the training and the governance documents as a yearly package. Platform runs your governance continuously, with live registers, staff sign-off and the evidence trail behind them. Consultancy is there when you want us to do the work with you.

A note on what we are. Attest AI is not a certification or accreditation body, and we do not certify organisations against any standard. We get you AI governance and compliance ready: the training, documents and evidence that support your journey, and that prepare you to work with an accredited body if you choose to pursue certification.
