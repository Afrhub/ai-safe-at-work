FAQ

If the course is free, why would we pay?

For the operational evidence. SCORM packaging so you can drop it into your LMS. Completion certificates with verifiable IDs so an auditor can see who actually finished. Sector overlays for your specific regulator. Quarterly update emails so you know when the standards moved. The course content is the proof of substance — the paid tier is the proof of deployment.

Will the free version always stay free?

Yes. It is doctrine. Any change to that would be a public, dated decision in our changelog, and the decision would have to survive a re-justification against the founding wedge. We do not foresee it.

Do you offer trials?

The free version IS the trial. If 12 modules + 2 Relevant Roles + 2 templates + the standards map + the changelog don't convince you it's worth paying for the operational layer, paying probably won't change that.

What about refunds?

30-day no-questions for individual purchases. Pro-rated refund for enterprise on cancellation. Specific terms will be in the commercial Terms of Service when launched.

Are you accredited?

Not yet. We are working on CPD/CPE recognition with IAPP, ISACA, and ISC2 (in that order of priority). Until that lands, individual certificates carry our own ID — verifiable on this site, accepted by buyers who don't require an accredited body, not yet recognised for formal CPD by professional associations.

Can I get an invoice / pay by bank transfer / use a purchase order?

Yes for Enterprise tier and above. Individual + Starter tiers will use card / instant payment for simplicity at launch.

What's the OPCO / Qualiopi route for French buyers?

Coming in Phase 4 (after Qualiopi certification). When live, French employers will be able to fund individual access for €399–€799 per seat via OPCO levy, and SMB / mid-enterprise bundles in a separate price band. The English-language Enterprise tiers above remain available in France in parallel.

Do you offer non-profit / academic discounts?

Yes — 30% off any tier for verified educational institutions and registered charities. Contact us with proof of status.
