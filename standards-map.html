EU AI Act — Regulation (EU) 2024/1689

Binding · phased 2025–2027

Article 4 is the literacy obligation in force today (since 2 February 2025). Every deployer must take "measures to ensure, to their best extent, a sufficient level of AI literacy of their staff and other persons dealing with the operation and use of AI systems". This course is one such measure.

ISO/IEC 42001:2023

Voluntary · certifiable AIMS

The world's first AI management-system standard. Clauses 7.2 (competence) and 7.3 (awareness), plus Annex A.4.5 (resources for AI systems including staff capability), are the human-capability spine. This course slots directly under them.
