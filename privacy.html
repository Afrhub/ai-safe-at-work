Privacy

Privacy.

A course about not leaking data should not leak yours either. This page is short on purpose.

What we collect

Nothing. There is no account system, no login, no analytics tracker, no advertising pixel, no remarketing cookie. The course is static HTML served as-is. We don't know who you are, and we don't want to.

What your browser does

Your browser fetches HTML, CSS and fonts. The fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which means Google sees the IP address your browser is using and the fact that a request for a font was made. We don't pass anything else to them and they don't tell us anything.

If you'd rather avoid that, configure your browser to block Google Fonts; the course pages remain legible in your browser's default system fonts.

What you put in

This is a one-way reading experience. There are no forms, no comments, no chat, no upload. Anything you type in your own browser stays in your own browser.

If you have a question

The course is intentionally not a service. If something on a page is wrong or out of date, please raise it via whichever channel you obtained the course through.
