Why AI Safe@Work?

People are already pasting customer details, contracts and source data into AI tools that nobody approved. One careless paste can leak private information, break a privacy law, or hand a scammer everything they need. Most of this "shadow AI" use is invisible to the business until something goes wrong.

The EU AI Act now expects organisations to give staff genuine AI literacy (Article 4, in force 2 February 2025). This is the wedge: a short, plain-English course that turns risky habits into safe ones and gives you the evidence that your people were trained.
