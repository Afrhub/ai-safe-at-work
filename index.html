AI Safe@Work

AI Governance Awareness + Enablement

Safe AI adoption.
Operational governance.

A free, plain-English course that teaches people how to use AI tools at work without leaking data, breaking the law, or getting fooled. Built for small and medium businesses and the MSPs who support them — and mapped to the EU AI Act, GDPR and the ISO standards behind the scenes.

Who it's for

Anyone at work who uses AI tools like ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude or Gemini — in sales, support, HR, finance, marketing or ops. You do not need to be techy. If you've ever pasted something into an AI and thought "should I have done that?", this is for you.

What it is

A short, free course — twelve small lessons, about 90 minutes in total. Each one shows you, in plain words, how to use AI at work the safe way. No login. Read it on the train, share it with your team.

Why it matters

One careless paste can leak a customer's private details, break a privacy law, or let a scammer trick the company. A few simple habits keep you, your customers and your business out of trouble.
