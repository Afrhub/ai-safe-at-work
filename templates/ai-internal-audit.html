AI Internal Audit

An internal audit is how you prove the governance pack is real, not just paperwork. Use this to plan the audit, work the checklist, log findings, and track fixes to closure. Aligns with ISO/IEC 42001 Clause 9.2.

Run at least once a year. The auditor should be independent of the work being audited.

i
Running the audit
  1. Pick an auditor who did not do the work being checked.
  2. Gather evidence, ask to see the filled-in registers and assessments, not just that they exist.
  3. Log every finding with an owner and a due date, then track to closure before the next cycle.
