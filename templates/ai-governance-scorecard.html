Red flags / escalations to leadership

Next month priorities

  1. Complete staff AI literacy training (drive to 95%+ completion).

  3. Assess shadow-AI tool identified this month.
  4. Conduct quarterly risk register review (standard cycle).

How to use this scorecard

  • Monthly brief to leadership: Present this scorecard in your monthly governance / risk review. Highlight red flags and green flags.
  • Trend tracking: Keep previous months' scorecards to see quarter-on-quarter trends (e.g. "training completion up 15% since January").
  • Accountability: Each red flag links to an owner and an action. Leadership can follow up: "What's the plan to get training completion to 100%?"
  • Audit evidence: A series of monthly scorecards is audit evidence that you're monitoring your governance maturity (ISO 42001 Clause 9.1).

Standards referenced

  • ISO/IEC 42001:2023, Clause 9.1 (monitoring and measurement of AI management system).
  • GDPR 2016/679, Article 32 (appropriate technical and organisational measures).
  • EU AI Act 2024/1689, Article 26 (ongoing monitoring of compliance).

Rolling this out
  1. Copy this template. Customise the seven dimensions and metrics to match your organisation's priorities.
  2. Collect data for each metric monthly (or quarterly). Source data from your registers (tool, risk, incident logs) and your training platform.
  3. Rate each metric Green (Pass) / Amber (At risk) / Red (Poor) based on whether you're on track to the target.
  4. Document any red flags: specific actions, owners, and due dates.
  5. Present to leadership monthly. Track trends over multiple months.
  6. File completed scorecards as audit evidence.
