Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment

Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment.

For deployers of high-risk AI systems where Article 27 applies, primarily public bodies, plus certain private deployers (e.g. those providing public services, banking creditworthiness scoring, life and health insurance risk assessment). Done before first use of the system.

Notify the market-surveillance authority of the FRIA result via the template form the AI Office will publish (Art 27(5)). File internally for audit.

Does Article 27 apply to you?

Article 27 applies if you are a deployer (not provider) of a high-risk AI system as listed in Annex III, and you fall into one of the following categories:

  • A body governed by public law, or a private entity providing public services.
  • A private deployer using high-risk AI to evaluate the creditworthiness of natural persons or establish their credit score (Annex III §5(b)).
  • A private deployer using high-risk AI for risk assessment and pricing in relation to life and health insurance (Annex III §5(c)).

If you do not fall into the above, Article 27 does not strictly mandate a FRIA, but conducting one is recognised good practice and supports broader Art 26 deployer duties, ISO/IEC 42001 risk processes, and supply-chain due diligence.
