7. Review schedule

DPIAs are not one-shot. Re-run on any of: change to scope, change to AI provider, change to data categories, change to retention, change to third-country posture, supervisory-authority guidance update, incident with the same vendor. Default review cadence: [Annual / At each material change / Quarterly for high-risk].

8. Standards mapping

  • GDPR — Articles 35 (DPIA), 36 (prior consultation), 30 (records of processing), 5 (principles), 6 (lawful basis), 9 (special categories), 22 (automated decisions), 32 (security), 28 (processors).
  • EU AI Act — Article 26(9) (deployer obligation to use DPIA results when AI system is high-risk and processes personal data); Article 27 (Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment — see FRIA template); Article 50 (transparency to natural persons interacting with AI).
  • ISO/IEC 42001:2023 — Annex A.5.4 (risk assessment for AI systems); Annex A.6.2.3 (data quality and management for AI).
  • ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — Annex A.5.34 (privacy and PII protection); Clause 6.1 (risk assessment).
  • ICO — DPIA guidance and template.
  • EDPB — WP248 rev.01 (Article 29 WP guidelines adopted by EDPB) on DPIAs.
