AI Risk Assessment

Run this once for each AI use case before you approve it, and again when the use case changes. Classify its EU AI Act risk tier, score likelihood and impact, record what you will do about it, then decide. Aligns with ISO/IEC 42001 Clause 6.1 (actions to address risks).

Complete one per use case. File it against the entry in your AI Use Case Register.

Section 2

EU AI Act risk tier

Tick the tier that fits. The tier sets the obligations, so be honest about it.

TierWhat it meansApplies?
ProhibitedBanned uses: social scoring, untargeted face-scraping, manipulation, most real-time biometric ID in public. If this fits, stop, do not deploy.Y / N
High-riskAnnex III uses: recruitment, worker management, access to essential services, credit, education, biometrics, critical infrastructure. Heavy duties apply.Y / N
Limited (transparency)Chatbots, generated content, emotion or biometric categorisation. Mainly a duty to tell people they are dealing with AI.Y / N
MinimalEverything else, spell-check, drafting help, summarising. No specific Act duties, but your own policy still applies.Y / N

i
How to use this
  1. Open one assessment per use case. Pull the use case straight from your AI Use Case Register.
  2. Set the tier first, it decides how much rigour the rest needs.
  3. Score honestly, then mitigate anything medium or high before you approve.
  4. File the completed assessment and set the review date in your calendar.
