AI Supplier Risk Assessment

Run this after the Supplier Due Diligence Questionnaire comes back. It turns the answers into a risk score and a decision: approve, approve with conditions, or walk away. Aligns with ISO/IEC 42001 Clause 8 and GDPR Article 28.

One per supplier. Re-run at renewal or when their service changes.

Section 2

Risk areas

Score each area 1 (low) to 5 (high), based on the due-diligence answers and evidence seen.

AreaWhat you are judgingScore 1–5
Data handlingWhere data goes, retention, and whether it trains their models–
SecurityCertifications (ISO 27001, SOC 2), encryption, access control, track record–
Sub-processorsWho else touches the data, and where they sit–
ComplianceGDPR Article 28 terms, DPA in place, EU AI Act obligations met–
TransparencyClear docs on how the model works, limits, and known failure modes–
Lock-in & exitCan you get your data out and leave without disruption–
ViabilityCompany stability, support quality, roadmap–

Overall: any single 5 is a red flag. A spread of 4s means conditions before you proceed.
