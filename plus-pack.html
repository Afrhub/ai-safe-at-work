Tier 2 · Governance documentation

The Plus Pack.

Training teaches your people. The Plus Pack gives you the paperwork to govern AI as an organisation: the policy, the registers, the assessments and the structure an auditor or a regulator expects to see. Every document is ready to fill in, prints to PDF, and is mapped to ISO/IEC 42001 and the EU AI Act.

The question it answers: "Do we have the documentation needed to start governing AI?"
